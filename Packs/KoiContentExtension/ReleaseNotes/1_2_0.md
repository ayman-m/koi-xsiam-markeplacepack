#### Incident Fields
##### New: KOI supply-chain incident fields
Added 19 incident fields for KOI supply-chain / agentic alerts, mapping to the parsing rule's
promoted columns and the Alert Triage / Investigate Item playbook outputs: item id/name/type/
version, marketplace (API form and raw event form), risk level, org risk level, alert type,
finding title, finding UID (policy), notification id (the de-duplication key), hostname, device
id/user, endpoint count (blast radius), governance state, triage verdict, and the investigation
summary.

#### Incident Types
##### New: KOI Supply Chain Alert
Added the **KOI Supply Chain Alert** incident type, bound to the **KOI Ext - Alert Triage** playbook
(autorun) and the **KOI Supply Chain Alert** layout.

#### Layouts
##### New: KOI Supply Chain Alert
Added the **KOI Supply Chain Alert** layoutscontainer (incident group): a "KOI Alert" tab grouping
item, risk & verdict, affected host, governance & identity, and the investigation summary, plus
standard Incident Info / Investigation / War Room tabs.

**Populating the fields:** the **KOI Ext - Alert Triage** playbook now populates all 19 fields via a `setAlert` task (after the triage summary, before verdict routing), mapping the KoiContext / KoiInvestigation / KoiTriage context it already computes -- including a 3-way governance label (blocklisted / allowlisted / ungoverned). No classifier or mapper is required.
